Weather is likely warm, but also unpredictable in late August, so come prepared! As for the level of formality, dress in such a way that makes you feel good. No bow-ties required, but accepted if you like bow-ties.


Who is invited/can I bring a guest?

We’ve listed the names of those invited in your group and/or any +1’s on the RSVP. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us!


Will there be activities for kids?

Yes, and a backyard playground! Also, a lot of the rec’s in the map above are family-friendly (it’s Utah after all)


How should I get to the venue?

There will be limited parking so please plan to uber/lyft to the venue. We are working on getting a shuttle from a nearby hotel, so stay tuned for more details on that.


Is there anything happening the next day?

Yes! If you’re around, we’ll be having some snacks in a nearby park the following morning. Let us know if you’re able to attend with the digital RSVP so we can get a rough headcount.


Do you have a registry?

In lieu of a registry, we are starting a honeymoon fund. If you wish to contribute towards our honeymoon, we ask you to please use our Venmo (@Nima-Shariat). If you would like to get us a physical gift, we would be grateful for any unique pieces you think we would enjoy!
